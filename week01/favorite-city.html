My Favorite City

Dubai will always be my favorite city — a place where the impossible feels routine. I love watching the sun set behind the Burj Khalifa, its glass spire catching gold light before the whole skyline flickers on for the night. There's something electric about a city that built itself out of desert sand and turned into one of the most futuristic places on Earth, all while keeping its heart in the old souks, where the smell of spices and the shimmer of gold still pull you back to Dubai's roots as a trading port. Whether I'm walking along the Marina at night, losing myself in the Gold Souk, or just watching the city hum with energy from a rooftop, Dubai reminds me that ambition and beauty can build something extraordinary.
